Image sizes

Because I'm tired of doing the math.

Why?

I got sick of calculating the scaled image for each breakpoint, so I wanted to create a simple little piece of html and JS that did it for you.

To use, put in your original image size, and then 1 or more breakpoints. When you click calculate and the corresponding image sizes will show up below.
